Ask someone in Bath!
Politely ask a local person (a shopkeeper, someone in the street, a museum guide) one or more of these questions in English. Tap what they tell you. If you can't find anyone to ask, make your best guess — we'll check the real answers in the debrief.
🙂 No photos of the person you ask — just the question and their answer. Only speak to people in public, in sight of your teacher.
1. Who is the Prime Minister of the UK right now?
2. About how many people live in Bath?
3. What is the name of the river that flows through Bath?
4. About how long ago did the Romans build the baths?
5. Besides the Roman Baths, what is Bath most famous for?

Bonus — for highly-motivated students

1. Find the number: what temperature is the spring water?
2. Why did the Romans throw coins and curses into the water? Write one sentence.
3. Describe one thing you saw today, in English, in your own words.
